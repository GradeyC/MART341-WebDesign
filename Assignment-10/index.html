A Brief History

Origionally released in 2008, Team Fortress 2(TF2) was an achivement in game design.
Being revolutionary for the time, it has had a longstanding impact on video games
even to this day.


Meet The Classes

Part of the reason TF2 is still so popular, 12 years after release is the classes.
The 9 different classes all work together to provide a much different and
refined experiance.

The Offense Classes


The Scout

The Scout is a lean mean running machine! Having a 33% higher base speed, and the ability to double jump The Scout was designed for capturing objectives.
His weapons make him deadly at mid-short range, with mobility to out manuver almost any opponet. He is not invinisible however, having only 125 health
it can make hard to stay alive.


The Soldier


The Soldier is a tough and strong class. Primarily using his rocket launcher, he can help take down senterys or clear the point.
He is also one of the slowest classes, only being beat out by The Heavy. However inspite of this The soldier is incredibly versitial.
This is thanks to rocket jumping, an action that pushes you around at the cost of health. To rocket jump you simply shoot a rocket at your feet.


The Pyro


Using the powers of fire and air nobody can defeat the mighty Pyro. Given the name for the iconic use of a flamethrower, the pyro is amazing offense class.
Having the ability to flank, find Spies and reflect projectiles, there are many roles for a pyro. Weither it be helping as a more agressive defender, or trying to
catch the enemy team off guard, a good pyro is an amazing addition to any team!


The Defense Classes


The Engineer


A brilient builder and amazing look out, the Engineer is a defense focused class.
Using a shotgun and being able to build everything from a sentery to a teleporter an Engineer can be
an amazing support. Being able to help teamates stay stocked up on ammo, health and even navagate the map faster
a good Engineer can make or break some games.


The Demoman


Bombs,grenades and swords what else could you ask for! The Demoman is a trap focused defense class, using his
sticky bombs to set up death traps for anyone unsuspecting to walk through them. Coupled with his pipe launcher,
The Demoman can bring about explosive mayhem!


The Heavy


He's tall, he's bulky and hes eating a sandwhich! The heavy is the final defense class, having a focus on pushing
objectives he is the slowest and tankiest character. With an amazingly strong minigun and 300 health taking a heavy down
can be a trial! He also have some supportive options, as The Heavy can use items that are food to heal and give his team mates health.

The Support Classes


The Medic


The Medic is an evil guiness of a healer! Using an amazing medi-gun, to continuously heal people and having the ability
to go invinicible with a team mate. The Medic can heavily sway the course of fights with his abilites. While being weaker than
many of the other classes, if protected he can greatly help make pushes and defending more successful!

The Sniper


The Spy


The Spy sneaking around and getting instant kills! The Spy is a support class mostly focused on going behind enemy lines.
Weather it is to take down a sentry, or take down the enemy heavy from the inside The Spy is made to decive people. Using his
disguise kit, he can make himself look like anyother class, and gaining some of their passive abilites.
